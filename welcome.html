Pic Sort (Black Break version) is a specialized tool designed for photographers, enabling fast and efficient image organization. It automatically detects black images, used as separators, and groups photos into categorized directories.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions you might have about our image categorization software. If you don't find your answer here, feel free to reach out to our support team.

How does the image categorization process work?

Simply select the images you want to categorize, start the process, and let the software work its magic. It analyzes your images and categorizes them into folders based on black or dark areas, which is useful for organizing event photos, competition shots, and more.

Can I rename the directories after the images are sorted?

Yes, once the images are categorized into directories, you can easily rename them based on your preference or the event’s specifics. The software is designed to give you full control over organization.

Is there a limit to the number of images I can process at once?

There is no hard limit on the number of images you can process at once, but larger batches may take longer to complete depending on your system's resources. We recommend processing in reasonable amounts for the best performance.

Do I need an internet connection to use the software?

No, the software is fully functional offline. You can use it to categorize images without any internet connection, which is ideal for photographers working in remote locations or during events.

What happens after the free trial ends?

After the 20-day free trial, you can choose to upgrade to a lifetime license or purchase the source code if you’d like to customize the software. Don’t worry, your image organization work will remain intact even if you decide not to continue.
